Incheon Global Campus Library aims to establishes a "earning community as a place for knowledge and cultural exchange between the East and the West"As a result, we are making efforts to expand and revitalize library books.

Incheon Global Campus would like to say thank you for the donation that helped to collect a high quality data to support academic research activities of students and faculty members of resident universities. The donated materials will be used preciously.

Registration Donation

  • Materials for academic research and educational purposes, especially domestic and foreign academic books, liberal arts books, and non-books (multimedia) related to the operation of the university’s curriculum

Restricted Donation

  • Books for examination, martial arts novel, workbooks, textbooks/studying materials for elementary, junior and high school, Recruitment Tests related books etc.

Method of Donation

  • Inquiry about donating materials → Submit a list of materials subject to donation → Decide whether to donate → Submit data donation → Proceed donation

    • Delivery (prepaid) or donor can submit by direct visit
    • In case of donation without the above procedure, unfortunately the library might not get the donated material
Application for Book Donation

Handling of Donation Data

  • In the case of 200 or more books donated, proceed after consultation with the person in charge according to the library's internal guidelines Decide whether to register the donated materials in the library collection according to the “Guidelines for the management of donated materials” If any of the donated item falls under the “item selection exclusion criteria”, it will not be registered and the library may process it arbitrarily.
